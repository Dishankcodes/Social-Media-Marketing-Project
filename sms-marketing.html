SMS Marketing Use Cases

How businesses leverage SMS for growth

Flash Sales & Promotions

Drive immediate action with limited-time offers. SMS creates urgency and generates quick sales spikes.

Example: "⚡ FLASH SALE! 50% off all items for the next 3 hours. Shop now: [link]"

Appointment Reminders

Reduce no-shows by 40% with automated appointment reminders sent 24 hours before scheduled time.

Example: "Hi Sarah, reminder: Your appointment is tomorrow at 2 PM. Reply C to confirm or R to reschedule."

Order Updates

Keep customers informed with real-time shipping notifications, delivery updates, and order confirmations.

Example: "Your order #12345 has shipped! Track it here: [link]. Expected delivery: Dec 25."

Birthday & Loyalty Rewards

Celebrate customer milestones with personalized offers that drive repeat purchases and build loyalty.

Example: "🎉 Happy Birthday John! Enjoy 20% off your next purchase. Use code: BDAY20"
